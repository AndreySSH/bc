The Ecume de mer
Author: Juergen Klemp

The Ecume de mer is a 1/4 ton calssic yacht constructed by Jean-Marie Finot, La Rochelle.
The author Juergen Klemp has sailed this boat from 1970 to 1975.

This is a classic yacht with great level of detail, beautifully created by Juergen Klemp (Lucho(at)t-online.de)
